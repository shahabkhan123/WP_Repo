Workout Routines

Full Body HIIT Workout

This high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workout is designed to improve your overall fitness, strength, and endurance.

Exercises:

  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Jump Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Upper Body Strength Training

This workout targets your chest, back, shoulders, and arms to build strength and muscle mass.

Exercises:

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 12 reps
