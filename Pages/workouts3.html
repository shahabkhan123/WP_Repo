Workout Routines

Core Strength Routine

This routine focuses on building core strength for better stability and posture.

Exercises:

  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 15 reps per side
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Flutter Kicks: 3 sets of 15 reps

Cardio Blast Workout

Increase your heart rate and burn calories with this intense cardio workout.

Exercises:

  • Treadmill Sprints: 4 sets of 1 minute
  • Cycling: 15 minutes at high intensity
  • Jump Rope: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps

Yoga for Flexibility

This yoga routine helps improve flexibility and reduce muscle tension.

Exercises:

  • Downward Dog: Hold for 30 seconds
  • Forward Bend: Hold for 30 seconds
  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 10 repetitions
  • Child's Pose: Hold for 1 minute
