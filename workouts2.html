Workout Routines

Kickboxing Workout

This kickboxing routine combines cardio and strength training to boost endurance and enhance coordination.

Exercises:

  • Jab-Cross Combo: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Front Kicks: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Roundhouse Kicks: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Squat Punches: 3 sets of 15 reps

Pilates Core Conditioning

This Pilates session focuses on core strength and flexibility, incorporating controlled movements for stability.

Exercises:

  • Hundred: 1 set of 100 pumps
  • Single-Leg Stretch: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Roll-Up: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank with Leg Lift: 3 sets of 8 reps per leg

Mobility and Flexibility Routine

This routine is designed to improve flexibility and joint mobility, ideal for recovery and overall wellness.

Exercises:

  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Hip Flexor Stretch: Hold for 30 seconds per side
  • Shoulder Stretch: Hold for 30 seconds per side
  • Seated Forward Bend: Hold for 1 minute
